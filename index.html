Automated Project Management at LG

Operations

The Problem

LG's project managemers were overwhelmed with the volume of projects, as well as the amount of teams they had to coordinate with. They spent most of their time running around the office trying to get scraps of information from teams.

The Solution

Using n8n and other integrations, built a representative for the 3D team with access to all of that team's databases and informationrunning on a local LLM model to coordinate directly with the project managers. In the future, all teams will have their own representative.

The Outcome

Reduced coordination costs by 80%. Error rates from communication dropped to near zero. Saved company ~$10k/year in labor hours.

Backend Automation for Cleaning Operations

Workflow Automation

The Problem

Managers handled data entry, photo uploads, and report creation by hand. Cleaners used clunky tools that created errors and slowed down the end-of-day workflow.

The Solution

Built a new backend system with automated data entry, automatic photo uploads to the database, and auto-generated client reports after each job. Also designed a clean interface for cleaners. Stack uses JavaScript, Vercel, and a modern backend.

The Outcome

Projected to save each manager at least an hour a day. Cleaner UX improves, reports send themselves, and errors from manual entry fade out.
